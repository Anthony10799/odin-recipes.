Description

This is a American cheeseburger wiht lettuce,tomato,onions,and white American cheese

Ingredients

  • buns
  • ground beef
  • lettuce
  • tomato
  • onions
  • ketchup
  • pickles
  1. In a bowl mix ground beef with onion powder,salt,and pepper.
  2. diviide meat into portions of desired patty size.
  3. Preheat pan on high heat.
  4. Cook until crust forms on both sides(about 2 minutes).When burges flip flip easily cook on other side for another 3 minutes.
  5. Put burger on buns with desired garnishes.
